Tourist Info

The Golden Gate Bridge in California connects to 2 cities in California, connecting between San Fransico & Sausalito. Around Sausalito, there is a place that is a Museum and its called the Bay Area Discovery Museum. Not to mention, the Bay Area Discovery Museum is known to be a "kids friendly museum". Families could have all the fun, visiting a Museum nearby along with capturing every memories of the Golden Gate Bridge since the Bay Area Discovery Museum is nearby the Golden Gate Bridge.
